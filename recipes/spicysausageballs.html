Spicy Sausage Balls

Description:

Spicy Sausage Balls are a great appetizer for any occasion. They are easy to make and can be made in advance and frozen for a later date. They are a great addition to any party or gathering.

Ingredients

  • sausage
  • Garlic powder
  • red pepper flakes
  • red wine vinegar
  • cream cheese
  • chedded cheese

Steps:

  1. Get all ingredients

    2.

    Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Large Bowl mix it

    3.

    Combine room temperature sausage and biscuit mix in a large bowl; mix with your hands until well combined.

  3. Add shredded cheese

    4.

    Add shredded Cheddar cheese and mix until fully incorporated.

  4. Roll the wheel

    5.

    Roll mixture into about 30 walnut-sized balls; transfer to a baking sheet.

  5. Bake

    6.

    Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown and sausage is cooked through, 20 to 25 minutes.
